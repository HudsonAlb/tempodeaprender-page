# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Parents (mothers and fathers) in Escada, Pernambuco and surrounding areas, looking for a school for their children (Early Childhood Education and Elementary School I). They are browsing on mobile or desktop, often comparing schools or returning as existing families. Many have limited digital experience, so navigation must be effortless and self-evident. They want to feel the school's warmth before visiting in person: a virtual tour of the heart of the institution.

## Product Purpose

Institutional website for Escola Tempo de Aprender, a school with 20+ years of history in Escada-PE. The site serves two goals: (1) convert prospective parents into enrolled families by building trust and making contact frictionless, and (2) strengthen the brand by demonstrating ongoing investment in the school's evolution, modernity, and commitment to quality education. Success looks like parents feeling confident enough to reach out after browsing, and existing families feeling proud to share the link.

## Brand Personality

Welcoming, dedicated, joyful.

The interface should evoke warmth and trust first, then surprise with polish and modernity. Parents should feel "this school cares deeply" within seconds. The tone is human and sincere, never corporate or distant. Joy comes through in color and motion, not through cartoonish excess.

## Anti-references

- Generic school websites built on cheap WordPress templates with clipart and stock icons
- Cold, corporate institutional sites that feel like government portals
- Overly childish designs drowning in primary-color chaos and cartoon characters
- Simple or flat minimum-viable pages that signal low investment
- Busy, cluttered layouts that overwhelm users with low digital literacy
- SaaS-style landing pages with metric grids and gradient CTAs

## Design Principles

1. **Show the heart, not the brochure.** Every section should feel like a window into the school, not a sales pitch. Authenticity over polish theater.
2. **Modern warmth.** Sophisticated design that still feels approachable. The lightness of a children's space expressed through contemporary visual language, not through infantile decoration.
3. **Effortless paths.** Every parent, regardless of tech comfort, should reach any destination in one or two obvious steps. Clarity is kindness.
4. **Earned attention.** Visual richness through purposeful craft (typography, spacing, subtle motion), not through volume or noise. Each element justifies its presence.
5. **Pride of place.** The site should make Escada-PE families proud. It should feel like it belongs to a school that is investing in being the best version of itself.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA baseline maintained. Primary audience has limited digital experience, so:
- Navigation must be obvious and forgiving (large touch targets, clear labels, visible focus states)
- Reading flow should be linear and predictable
- Reduced motion respected via `prefers-reduced-motion`
- Color contrast ratios enforced across all text and interactive elements
- Forms must be simple with clear error states and guidance
